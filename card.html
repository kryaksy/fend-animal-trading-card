Haikouichthys

Haikouichthys is about 2.5 cm (0.98 in) long and is narrower than Myllokunmingia, another putative chordate that comes from the same beds.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Superclass: Agnatha

Haikouichthys is an extinct genus of animals with notochords and distinct heads, believed to have lived 525 million years ago
